Aqua Vortex

Spin, swirl, and splash in the ultimate water funnel experience

About the Ride

The Aqua Vortex is a giant funnel water slide that pulls riders into a thrilling spiral before dropping them into a splash pool. Riders twist around the vortex walls at high speed, creating a mix of weightless moments and powerful g-forces. This ride is perfect for groups and solo thrill seekers alike!

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum height: 110 cm (3 ft 7 in).
  • Maximum combined weight for 2 riders: 180 kg (397 lbs).
  • Riders must sit properly in tubes at all times.
  • No loose articles, food, or drink allowed.
  • Guests with recent surgery, pregnancy, or heart/back/neck conditions should not ride.

When It Was Built

The Aqua Vortex was introduced in 2020 as part of the “Family Thrills” expansion. Its unique funnel design was engineered in Germany and shipped piece by piece to Broadbeach, where it became an instant favorite.

Fun Facts

  • The funnel is 18 meters (59 ft) wide—big enough to fit a bus inside!
  • It’s one of the few rides where you can choose solo or double tubes.
  • Special lighting effects make the funnel glow purple at night.
