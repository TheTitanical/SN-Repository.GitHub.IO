Tropical Twister

Spin, swirl, and splash through a whirlwind of fun

About the Ride

The Tropical Twister is a spiraling water slide that sends riders twisting and turning down vibrant, sunlit tubes before plunging into a splash pool. With glowing tropical colors and sharp spirals, this ride combines speed with dazzling scenery for an unforgettable thrill.

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum height: 115 cm (3 ft 9 in).
  • Maximum weight: 120 kg (265 lbs).
  • Riders must keep arms and legs inside the slide at all times.
  • No jewelry, goggles, or loose accessories allowed.
  • Guests with recent injuries or heart/back problems should not ride.

When It Was Built

The Tropical Twister opened in 2019 as part of the park’s “Color Splash Expansion.” Its bold orange-and-yellow design makes it stand out, while its fast spirals earned it a reputation as one of the most exhilarating slides in the park.

Fun Facts

  • The slide features over 6 full spirals before reaching the splash pool.
  • At night, colored LED lights make the slide glow like a neon spiral.
  • It’s one of the most photographed rides in Splash! Express due to its bold design.
