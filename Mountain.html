Splash Mountain Slide

Race down the mountain’s edge with roaring water all around

About the Ride

The Splash Mountain Slide is a towering adventure that combines steep drops, winding turns, and a dramatic final splash. Modeled after natural mountain waterfalls, this ride takes guests on a twisting descent from the summit, giving both breathtaking views and heart-pounding thrills.

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum height: 125 cm (4 ft 1 in).
  • Maximum weight: 130 kg (286 lbs).
  • Riders must remain seated and cross arms/legs when sliding.
  • No loose items, cameras, or goggles permitted.
  • Not recommended for guests with heart or back conditions.

When It Was Built

Splash Mountain Slide was unveiled in 2021 as the centerpiece of the “Adventure Peaks” expansion. Its design blends concrete mountain facades with flowing waterfalls to make guests feel like they’re racing down a natural gorge.

Fun Facts

  • The slide is over 200 meters long, making it one of the park’s longest rides.
  • It uses over 3 million liters of water every day to power its flow.
  • The mountain structure includes hidden tunnels with special sound effects for added immersion.
