Rapid River Rapids

Brace yourself for a roaring adventure through turbulent waters

About the Ride

The Rapid River Rapids is a fast-flowing whitewater adventure that simulates the thrill of rafting through wild rapids. Climb aboard a circular raft with friends and family as you spin, splash, and crash through swirling waves, waterfalls, and hidden twists around every bend.

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum height: 110 cm (3 ft 7 in).
  • Maximum raft capacity: 6 riders.
  • Seat belts must be worn at all times.
  • No standing or switching seats during the ride.
  • Guests with heart, back, or neck problems should not ride.

When It Was Built

The Rapid River Rapids opened in 2017 and was built to replicate the excitement of whitewater rafting in a safe, family-friendly environment. It quickly became a signature attraction at Splash! Express and continues to thrill thousands of riders every year.
