Wave Rider Pool

Catch the waves and ride the surf without leaving Broadbeach

About the Ride

The Wave Rider Pool is our giant wave simulator, creating rolling swells that mimic the ocean. From gentle ripples to towering waves, it’s the perfect spot for both relaxation and adventure. Whether you’re floating in the shallows or diving into the deep end, every session feels like a trip to the beach.

Safety Requirements

  • No minimum height for shallow areas; 120 cm (4 ft) required for deeper waves.
  • Life jackets available and recommended for non-swimmers.
  • No diving or rough play allowed.
  • Children under 8 must be supervised by an adult.
  • Follow all lifeguard instructions at all times.

When It Was Built

The Wave Rider Pool opened in 2016 as one of the first attractions in the park. Inspired by Gold Coast beaches, it was designed to bring the ocean to Broadbeach, offering waves every 15 minutes in a controlled and family-friendly environment.

Fun Facts

  • The pool generates waves up to 1.5 meters high during peak sessions.
  • Over 1 million liters of water are in constant circulation.
  • At night, synchronized lights make the waves glow like moonlit tides.
