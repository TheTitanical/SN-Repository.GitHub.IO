Lazy Lagoon

Relax, float, and drift away in our sun-soaked paradise

About the Ride

The Lazy Lagoon is the calm heart of Splash! Express. Grab an inflatable tube and gently float along a winding river surrounded by palm trees, waterfalls, and hidden grottoes. Perfect for families and those who prefer a relaxing experience between the thrill rides.

When It Was Built

Lazy Lagoon opened in 2015 and was one of the original attractions at Splash! Express. Designed as a tropical retreat, it remains a guest favorite for its peaceful atmosphere and scenic design.

Fun Facts

  • The river stretches over 500 meters, making it the longest attraction in the park.
  • Over 40,000 inflatable tubes are used each year by guests.
  • At night, floating lanterns light up the lagoon, creating a magical glow.
