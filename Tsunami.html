The Tsunami Drop

Experience the tallest and fastest slide at Splash! Express

About the Ride

The Tsunami Drop is the crown jewel of Splash! Express, standing at an incredible 35 meters tall. Riders plummet almost vertically down a near-straight slide before splashing into a massive pool below. The free-fall sensation combined with the refreshing splash makes this our most thrilling attraction.

Safety Requirements

  • Minimum height: 120 cm (4 ft).
  • Maximum weight: 120 kg (265 lbs).
  • Riders must be able to lie flat with arms crossed over the chest.
  • No loose clothing, jewelry, or unsecured items allowed.
  • Guests with heart conditions, back/neck issues, or pregnancy should not ride.

When It Was Built

The Tsunami Drop opened in 2018 as part of our “Extreme Thrills Expansion.” Designed by award-winning engineers, it remains one of the tallest body slides in Queensland and continues to attract adrenaline seekers from around the world.

Fun Facts

  • The average rider reaches speeds of up to 70 km/h (43 mph)!
  • Over 500,000 riders have braved the drop since opening day.
  • At night, the slide lights up with LED waves, creating a glowing tsunami effect.
